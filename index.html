Tour Overview

The T&P Connections Tour is our annual TPIL initiative that brings together employees across all levels to experience our strategic priorities and latest technological advancements in a meaningful way. It creates space for teams to connect, collaborate, and learn from one another—helping us build stronger relationships and drive innovation across the organization.

RADX Partnership

Roles & Expectations

Collaborative partnership model to ensure high-quality, engaging content and strong leadership presence

Executive Sponsors

Help drive visibility, credibility, and leadership engagement across the tour

  • Actively promote the tour within their organizations and networks
  • Attend tour stops to show visible support
  • Engage in leadership networking sessions
Ask: Sponsor Hyderabad & Bengaluru tour stops & help champion the overall program
Subject Matter Experts

Play a critical role in shaping high-quality, relevant content for the tour

  • Partner to co-create session content aligned to tour objectives
  • Pilot content ahead of the tour, incorporating feedback
  • Ensure all materials are finalized and ready by June 5
Ask: Identify SMEs to collaborate on shared content
Speakers

Key to delivering an engaging and consistent experience across hubs

  • Commit to presenting at a minimum of two tour stops
  • Deliver content that is interactive, clear, and tailored to the audience
  • Incorporate feedback from early sessions to continuously improve delivery
Ask: Identify engaging session & booth speakers to join us at relevant Tour stops
